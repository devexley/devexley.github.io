Impressions

About DBC

June 11, 2015

DBC is a very intensive experience and requires a lot of yourself. I'm usually very solitary but think this would be a good program to be part of a learning community and be creative. I hope to be able to contribute to the holistic program.

My expectations have been changed somewhat as I realize I need to be more participatory. This is difficult for me but I think I am up to the challenge.

I am nervous still for a variety of reasons. Will I be good enough and learn fast enough. I have to get over these fears and try to be as much a part of the program as I can to get the most out of it. I look forward to the challenge
